ATLAS of LIGHTING userTest
ABOUT

The purpose of this set of visualizations is to allow users to explore and better understand the role of lighting in different types of urban areas across the United States. The visualizations compare geolocated quality of life data with measurements of lighting.



The map and chart to the left plots the Metropolitan Statistical Areas selected to be included in future implementations of the atlas for comparison. These MSAs are color coded according to a previous designation by the CAU into 5 general groups.
